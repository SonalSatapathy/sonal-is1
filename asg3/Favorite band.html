Stray Kids

Stray Kids is a boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. They were formed through the 2017 JYP Entertainment reality show called "Stray kids", with leader Bang Chan handpicking the final nine members. The group debuted on March 25, 2018, with the digital single "District 9", but currently consists of eight members after Woojin's departure in 2019. Known for their self-produced music through the subunit 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han), Stray Kids are a successful fourth-generation K-pop group, having achieved major chart success including topping the Billboard 200. The band consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N.
