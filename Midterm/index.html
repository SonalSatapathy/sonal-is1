Osaka

Osaka is a major Japanese tourist destination known for its lively atmosphere, incredible food, and blend of modern and historic attractions. Key draws include the bustling Dotonbori area, Osaka Castle, Universal Studios Japan, and the city's reputation as a culinary hub, often called "The Nation's Kitchen". Its friendly and outgoing locals also contribute to its reputation as a fun and welcoming city to explore. Learn more about fun things to do by clicking on the above tabs.
